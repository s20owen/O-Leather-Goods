Care & longevity
  • Full grain leather will darken and soften with use (this is the good part).
  • Keep it away from heavy soaking — if it gets wet, let it dry naturally.
  • Occasional conditioning helps, but don’t overdo it.
  • Scratches usually blend with time and patina.
Repairs and adjustments are often possible — reach out if something needs attention.

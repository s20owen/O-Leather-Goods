The Workshop

Each piece is crafted in small batches in our workshop using traditional leatherworking tools — knives, stitching irons, edge tools, and hand presses. This slower approach allows careful control over every detail of the final product.

Bespoke Philosophy

Every piece begins with a conversation. Rather than producing goods in bulk, we craft each item individually based on how it will be used. The goal is simple: create leather goods that fit naturally into your everyday life — whether that means a wallet carried daily or a bag built for years of travel.

Material Selection

We work primarily with full-grain vegetable-tanned leather sourced from some of the most respected tanneries in the world. In the United States, this includes historic makers such as Wickett & Craig and Hermann Oak, both known for producing traditional vegetable-tanned leather with rich depth and durability.

We also incorporate leather from renowned Italian tanneries located in the Tuscan leather district — including Badalassi Carlo, and Conceria Walpier. These tanneries are part of a centuries-old tanning tradition that focuses on slow vegetable tanning methods that create leather with exceptional character and longevity.

Vegetable tanning uses natural tannins derived from tree bark and plants, a slower process that produces leather capable of developing a deep patina over time rather than wearing out. These materials are valued for their strength, natural surface, and ability to age beautifully.

Traditional Hand Stitching

All pieces are stitched entirely by hand using the traditional saddle stitch method. Two needles pass through each hole from opposite sides of the leather, creating a seam that is exceptionally strong. Unlike machine stitching, a saddle-stitched seam will not unravel if a thread is damaged.

This method takes longer than machine sewing, but it allows greater control over every stitch and produces a stronger, longer-lasting product.

Cutting & Edge Finishing

Each piece is individually cut edges are beveled, burnished, and sealed by hand to create a smooth finish that improves with age and use.

Built to Age Well

Leather is one of the few materials that becomes more beautiful with time. Instead of hiding wear, our pieces are designed to embrace it. The surface develops a patina, colors deepen, and the leather gradually molds to the way you use it.

Made to Order

Most items are produced after an order is placed. This allows us to focus on craftsmanship rather than inventory and ensures that every piece is made with care from start to finish.
